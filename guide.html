What is Rankup?

Rankup is a powerful Discord bot designed to manage role hierarchies in your server. It automates rank management and empowers your members to promote others based on permissions you configure, removing the burden from admins while keeping a clear chain of command.

How It Works

1

Define Your Hierarchy

Set up your rank structure using simple commands. Define each rank in order from lowest to highest. You can create multiple rank groups (Staff, Members, VIP) that work independently.

2

Delegate Promotions

Stop doing everything yourself! Grant specific ranks permission to promote others. For example, allow Captains to promote Recruits. They use /up @user, and the bot handles the permissions safely.

3

Auto-Update Nicknames

Enable nickname sync to automatically add rank prefixes. When someone gets promoted, their nickname updates from "[Private] John" to "[Sergeant] John" instantly.

4

Secure Hierarchy Protection

Built-in security prevents abuse. Members can't promote others above their own rank, and the bot enforces your hierarchy rules automatically.

5

Manage Sub-Roles

Link multiple roles to a single rank. Promoting someone can automatically give them access roles, channel permissions, or special perks all at once.
